About Us

14 years of evolution and a passionate commitment to research the beauty and oddness have created the unique, rich and layered identity that Abhika shows today. Kassa Furniture provides comfort, awe, elegance and fun altogether. A total look to dress up your space and have your guests wondering if their inner curiosity has just showed up. The strenuous attention to our products quality, focused both on the choice of materials and the details, is complemented by the precious work of the independent, hand-picked artisans that team up with us to create and customize durable, contemporary goods of the utmost quality.
